<h2 class="section-title">About me</h2>
<section class="about">
  <p>I'm a founding member of <strong>RaucherPauseGames</strong> — a small collective that started by collaborating on a 2023 game jam and has been making games together ever since.</p>
  <p>I create engaging experiences by finding original ways to pose problems and design solutions. As a creative and designer I've learned that the best work comes from a long process of iteration and validation.</p>
  <p>I value <strong>transparency and openness</strong>, building connections that last, and avoiding stagnation and waste. I care about fun, efficiency, and dedication — outstanding results come from engaged and happy groups of people.</p>
  <p>Always learning. I play all kinds of games — physical and digital — always looking for the next mechanic or idea that inspires me. Always on the hunt for interesting subjects that can be turned into engaging games and stories.</p>
</section>
