## Game Design

Creating the mechanics, dynamics, and actions that make a multiplayer rover game feel good. Key areas I owned:

- **Multiplayer interactions** — designing around the chaos of 4+ players competing and cooperating at once
- **Economy design** — resource flow, scoring, and how player decisions compound over a match
- **Level design** — playable spaces that reward different playstyles
- **Vehicle design** — rovers that feel responsive and fun to drive

## Game Art

Creating 3D assets for the world:

- **Vehicles & tools** — every rover, drill, scanner
- **Environments** — alien biomes, base structures
- **Texturing** — PBR workflows in Substance

## Tech Art

- **Dynamic landscape material** that reacts to player activity
- **Post-processing FX** for item interactions and visual feedback
- **Rigging & animation** in Unreal's toolset

## Project

A team-of-two project where I wore the design, direction, and art hats. Beyond game work, I also handled:

- Agile project management
- Team lead responsibilities
- Networking implementation
- Social media presence and community updates
