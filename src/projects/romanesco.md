## Project

*Romanesco* started as a 48-hour game jam with the theme *"shell"*. The original team came back a year later to continue working on the game, eventually forming **RaucherPauseGames** as an independent game-dev collective.

We were lucky to present the game at **Gamescom 2025 — Home of Indies**, and we even made some merch for both the game and the collective.

## Game Art

- **Fractal artwork** — procedural visuals inspired by Romanesco broccoli
- **Graphic design** — UI, menus, and identity
- **Screen effects** — glitch, scanlines, the cyberspace vibe

## Game Design

- **Level design** — handcrafted arenas
- **Procedural generation** — endless run-to-run variety
- **Difficulty progression** — escalates the further you go
- **Score economy** — risk/reward balancing for speedrunners chasing the world record

## Success

A jam project that became a real game, a team, and a collective. Worth all the late nights.
