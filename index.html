About me

The Craft

I create engaging experiences by finding original ways to create problems and solutions. In my role as creative and designer I have learned that the best work comes from a long process of iteration and validation.

Teamwork

Transparence and openness, looking to create value, connections that last - avoiding stagnation & waste production. I value fun, efficiency and dedication. Outstanding results come from engaged and happy groups of people.

Curiosity

Playing all kinds of games, physical and digital, always looking for the next mechanic or idea that inspires me. Always learning about everything - finding interesting subjects that can be turned into engaging games and stories.
